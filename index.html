ECWA MISSION

To transform church and society by winning all for Christ through sound biblical teaching, holistic ministry, and dynamic leadership development.
The ECWA's mission statement emphasizes three key areas:

  • Sound biblical teaching: The Bible is the inspired word of God and that it provides the foundation for all of its teachings and practices.
  • Holistic ministry: The gospel addresses the whole person, not just the spiritual needs. It is involved in a wide range of ministries, including healthcare, education, and community development.
  • Dynamic leadership development: It is important to equip leaders with the skills and knowledge they need to effectively lead the church and its ministries.

The ECWA's mission statement is a reflection of its core values:

  • Faithfulness to Scripture: The Bible is the ultimate authority for its faith and practice.
  • Integrity: Committed to living according to the highest ethical standards.
  • Servanthood:Christians are called to serve others.

ECWA VISION

  • Spreading Christianity: A society where a significant portion of the population adheres to their Christian beliefs.
  • Transforming society: A better world built on Christian principles.
  • Holistic ministry: The church actively addresses social, economic, and educational needs beyond pure spiritual guidance.
  • Strong leadership: Where competent leaders guide the church towards its goals.
